Rania Sioufas Bookkeeping

A Competent Bookkeeping Service Company

We provide professional bookkeeping services to small and medium sized companies whose current bookkeeping needs are not being met. Our staff provides bookkeeping services in a timely and accurate manner while showing the utmost respect and awareness of the confidentiality and individual needs of our client. We're here to help you run your business more efficiently and in the process save you both time and money. You pay for the services that you need, when you need them. No payroll taxes, no employee benefits, no office overhead.

Rania's Bookkeeping is head-quartered in Westchester, NY and provides bookkeeping services to clients in New York and Connecticut.
